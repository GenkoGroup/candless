Handmade soy wax candle sculpted into a beautiful statue of Venus de Milo. Greek goddess roman statue design. Our bust candles are the perfect size to keep on your mantlepiece or somewhere special.

Made from soy wax, they are vegan and come in plastic-free packaging.

We’re a big fan of using these as decor but if you’re keen to light her up then we’d recommend popping a large dish underneath as she can get quite messy. Burn time is roughly 45 mins.

Every candle is hand-poured individually so you can expect little imperfections such as air bubbles and small blemishes - we think this adds character! It’s also normal for soy wax to acquire a ‘frosty/dusty’ layer over time which you can remove by buffing gently with your fingers or a soft cloth. The colour is a creamy off white.
