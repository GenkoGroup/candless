FAQ

Why soy wax?

Soy wax has numerous health and environmental benefits. Some of the benefits include:

  • It is a natural wax option, unlike paraffin wax that may contain toxins
  • Contains no petrol-soot that will blacken walls or ceilings
  • Has a longer lasting burn
  • Derived from a renewable resource (soy beans)
  • Biodegradable and easy to clean-up with soap and water

What is your return policy?

The Customer has the right to retract his order without reason in the fourteen days (14) after receiving his order. This date will be determined by the transporter’s receipt signed by the Customer. When this 14 day lapse of time ends on a Saturday, a Sunday or a State holiday, it is automatically prolonged until the next Working day. To use his right to retract, the Customer must send via email or postal mail, a non-ambiguous declaration, that expresses his will to retract. This declaration must be sent with the information concerning the products and their quantities returned to UniversalSculpture Co.™ as well as the postal address, the order number and if possible the phone number and the email address of the Customer to the following address: UniversalSculpture Co.™ Koński Jar 2 / 4 02-785 Warsaw Poland Products must be returned 14 days at the latest after delivery. The return shipping costs will be at the Client’s expense. Every product must be return unused, in its original packaging, unopened and undamaged. UniversalSculptuyre will not reimburse or exchange Products returned by the Customer that do not correspond to the products ordered or that were not ordered on UniversalSculptuyre’s Online Store.

What happens if I don't collect my package?

In the event that products are returned to us due to customer failing to pick up their package, we charge a €35 euro fee. This covers shipping to and from you, as well as handling and restocking of products.

What if the order is lost in the mail?

For packages lost in transit, all claims must be submitted no later than 4 weeks after the estimated delivery date. But no worries! We'll cover the costs of shipping a replacement order for you. We may ask for your help before doing that, like confirming that the shipping address was correct. It would also be good to double check that you got in touch with your local post office to try locating the lost order. Keep in mind that if tracking information states an order was delivered but you say you haven't received it, we won't take responsibility and reship that order. In that case, any replacements would have to be at your expense.

My merchandise was damaged when I received it; can I get a shipping refund?

When your order arrives, please inspect carton for damage that may have occurred during shipment. It is normal for shipping carton to show some wears, however, if substantial damage occurred to the item(s) in your shipment, please do not accept the package. Once the package has been returned to us and we have verified the damage, we will organize for a replacement to be sent to you.
